WINGS OF PREY

Welcome to 'Wings of Prey,' where the vast expanse of the sky is your playground. In this immersive flight experience, players are invited to take control of a variety of aircraft and embark on thrilling aerial adventures. Whether flying solo in the Career Mode, engaging in cooperative missions with friends, or testing your skills in adrenaline-pumping 1 vs 1 battles, every moment in 'Wings of Prey' is an exhilarating journey through the clouds. With its blend of cooperative gameplay, strategic dogfights, and breathtaking vistas, 'Wings of Prey' offers an unforgettable flight experience that celebrates the freedom and excitement of aviation.

Chanderelle

Welcome to 'Chanderelle'! Immerse yourself in an exhilarating world of poisonous mushrooms, where cunning and skill reign supreme. Developed in JavaScript, 'Chanderelle' invites you to a frantic showdown of shooting and platforming in an exciting 1 vs 1 duel. Take on a friend in intense local battles where every move counts, and every platform can be both your sanctuary and your downfall. With its vibrant design and fast-paced gameplay, 'Chanderelle' delivers a unique gaming experience where strategy and reflexes collide in an thrilling duel for supremacy in the realm of poisonous fungi. Plus, utilizing WebSockets technology, 'Chanderelle' supports multiplayer functionality, allowing players to engage in heated battles over the internet, adding an extra layer of excitement to the mushroom-filled mayhem.
