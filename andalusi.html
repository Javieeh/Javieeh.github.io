ANDALUSÍ

Andalusí project, rooted in the vibrant city of Seville, draws inspiration from its rich cultural heritage and architectural marvels. Originating from Seville myself, I've meticulously crafted this project using a combination of cutting-edge design software including SketchUP, 3ds Max, and Blender. These powerful tools have allowed me to intricately model the essence of Andalusian aesthetics, capturing the essence of its iconic structures, intricate details, and timeless charm. Through this project, I aim to showcase the beauty and allure of Seville's architectural wonders, paying homage to its captivating history and cultural significance.

Incorporating authenticity into every detail, numerous textures utilized in this project are derived from original photographs captured by myself. Through meticulous composition and keen observation, I've ventured to encapsulate the essence of various surfaces, from weathered bricks to intricate tiles, through the lens of my camera. Leveraging the capabilities of Photoshop, these raw images underwent a transformative process, meticulously tiled and meticulously treated to seamlessly integrate into the project's digital realm. This hands-on approach not only ensures a genuine representation of textures but also imbues each element with a personal touch, fostering a deeper connection to the project's narrative and origins.
